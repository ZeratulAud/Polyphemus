Nate Hatch

For my Senior Project we are updating and adapting a codebase given to us by Dr. Alireza Tavakkoli. This codebase is called the VETO Codebase. This codebase contains all the necessary components to perform teleoperation on remote robots by utilizing VR controls and game engine renders. The codebase does its rendering with Unreal Engine 4 and operates control from within an Oculus Rift environment. Our project entails us updating the codebase to work with a new robot with new sensors, two new physical locations, updated software, and new objects that we will manually model into digital to-scale objects.
